Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers! Check out our FAQs below to learn more about Matcha Sketch.

FAQs

What is Matcha Sketch?

Matcha Sketch is a platform where creativity meets personalization. We create unique, custom profile pictures and pixel art designs tailored to your vision.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by signing up for an account, browsing our gallery for inspiration, and submitting a request through our order form.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods.

How long does it take to receive my design?

Design turnaround time typically ranges from 3-7 business days, depending on the complexity of the request.

Can I request revisions?

Yes! We offer a certain number of free revisions depending on the package you choose. We want to ensure your satisfaction with the final design.

How can I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to us via our contact form or email us at support@matchasketch.com.
